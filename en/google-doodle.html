/Google Doodle

Illustration

A Doodle it’s used to give a temporary personality to the Google’s Logo in order to celebrate some event or important date. This Doodle is inspired by the launch of the 25th season of The Simpsons in the United States. I’ve made use of the physical characteristics of the main characters to adapt them to the Google’s logo and ensure to identify it with the desired personality.
